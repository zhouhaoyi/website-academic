I am Haoyi Zhou (周号益), currently an assistant professor in the College of Software at Beihang University, also with Beijing Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Computing (BDBC) in the ACT lab research unit.

My current research interests are in artificial intelligence and machine learning research, with a focus on the application of time-series forecasting, task decision-making, AI safety, and other technologies in the industrial Internet and medical care. We forge the BUAA-SMART group. If you are eager for the similar research vision, please feel free to reach me.

    Projects

    NSFC, PI, Research on Long Sequence Forecasting for Industrial Big Data, 2022
    Big data technology is widely applied in industrial digital transformation, and the growth in both the length and scale of sequence data raises the need for long sequence analysis. However, the traditional methods have severe long-term prediction failure and other problems. Based on the leading achievements in our proposed self-attention mechanism, this project plans to focus on the long sequence problems.
