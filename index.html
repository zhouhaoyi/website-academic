I am a assistant professor in the College of Software at Beihang University, also with Beijing Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Computing (BDBC), in the ACT lab research unit.

My current research interests are in artificial intelligence and machine learning research, with a focus on the application of time-series analysis, image recognition, and other technologies in the industrial Internet and medical care.

Previously, I have received a bachelor's degree from Beihang University in 2013. I was a PhD student at Beihang University, supervised by Professor Jianxin Li, also worked as a visiting schorlar with Professor Xiong Hui at Rutgers University in 2020.
